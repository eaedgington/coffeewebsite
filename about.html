How was my experience with Harp and SSGs so far? What worked out well? What didn't? Would you use Harp for future work?

My experience with Harp so far is good, but I would be lying if I said I'm not a little intimidated. I followed along with the instructions and achieved the downloads, I had no real issues. I think that knowing how to use Harp will be useful in the future.

An example of something that I could make with Harp

I would be interested in using Harp for making a blog! Harp is known for it's abilities to make blogs. And the only thing I want to make a blog about is my plant obsession! I am currently making a blog about plants for another class and I am interested in the comparison of Harp and WordPress.com
